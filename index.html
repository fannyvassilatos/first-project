About Vassani

Vassani Stay is located in the center of Canggu, a cool surfer/yoga/healthy restaurant/village. Vassani Stay is an eco friendly guesthouse located on Jl Munduk Catu between Batu Bolong and Echo beach, 400m from the beach.
